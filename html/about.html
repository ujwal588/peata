About Peata

At Peata, we are passionate about pets. Our goal is to provide the highest quality care and services to keep your furry friends happy, healthy, and safe. Whether it's grooming, boarding, or veterinary services, we are committed to treating your pets like family.

Our Mission

To offer a full range of pet care services delivered by trained professionals who love animals just as much as you do.

Our Vision

To be the leading pet care provider in the community, known for excellence in customer service and compassion for pets.

Meet Our Team

Our dedicated team includes experienced veterinarians, skilled groomers, and friendly staff, all here to ensure your pets receive the care they deserve.
